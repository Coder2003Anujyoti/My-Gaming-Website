Stone Paper Scissors

Stone Paper Scissors is a simple hand game where Stone crushes Scissors, Scissors cuts Paper, and Paper covers Stone. It is a quick, fun way to make decisions and demonstrates cyclic dominance in game theory.

Handcricket Game

Hand Cricket with 10 wickets involves players using hand movements to simulate batting and bowling. Each team has 10 wickets to defend, and the team with the most runs when all wickets are lost wins.It is an International version.

Tic Tac Toe

Tic Tac Toe is a simple game where two players take turns marking a 3x3 grid with Xs and Os. The goal is to align three of your marks in a row, column, or diagonal. The first to achieve this wins.

Memory Game

A memory game typically involves a set of cards or tiles laid face down, with each card or tile having a matching pair. The goal is to find all the matching pairs by flipping two cards at a time and remembering their locations. It helps improve concentration and memory skills.
